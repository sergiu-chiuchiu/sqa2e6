FAQ


What ingredients are used and what are their nutritional values?

We use only genuine and high quality ingredients. Every tab of our pizza lists the specific ingredients. Concerning yeast, classic pizzas use starter yeast while brewer's yeast is used for gluten free pizzas. The product cards found on our web site contain tabs listing the nutritional values.

Can any of the pizza ingredients be changed or eliminated?

Yes, you can specify if you want to change or eliminate the ingredients you want, or you can simply can customize your pizza.

How do I order a pizza?

You can buy out pizza by simply click on the button Add to cart after you see out menu.

Do I have to order a minimum number of pizzas?

No, you can order just one pizza if you want.

Can I order differnet types of pizzas in the same order?

Yes. You can also order drinks and dips on the same order.

How much is delivery?

If you order less than 50 Lei, the delivery will cost 10 Lei if your address is in the city and 15 Lei if your address is out of city. If your adress is in the city, if you order more than 50 Lei, the delivery is free and if your adress is out of the city you must order more the 100 Lei for a free delivery.

How can I pay?

You can pay online by PayPal or credit/ debit card or cash at the moment you receive the order.

Do you deliver every day?

Yes, but in the weekend we deliver until 11 PM and in the rest of the week until 1 AM

How long is delivery time?

It depends on the traffic, meteo conditions and the distance between the restaurant and delivery address, but usually 1-2 hours.

Can I arrange for a specific delivery time?

Yes, you can specify that before you sent the order.
