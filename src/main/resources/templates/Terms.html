Minimum Order


In order to qualify for free delivery you must spend 40 LEI or more. Orders of less than 50 LEI do not qualify for delivery and therefore you must pay the delivery tax.

Delivery Area


We aim to provide free delivery to the City area. However, in cases where the delivery address is more than 10 kms from the Pizza Mia premises, additional charges may apply if the order is less than 100 LEI. You will be informed of these charges and a cash payment can be made upon delivery.


Delivery times


We aim to delivery your pizza as quickly as possible. Unfortunately things do not always go to plan and factors, such as, weather, staff illness and traffic occasionally prevents us from achieving this. During Sunday to Thursday, we are unable to accept online orders after 11pm. On Fridays and Saturdays, we are unable to accept online orders after 1am.
